Incorporating Fun

Although academics are your #1 priority, you want to make sure that you are participating in events on campus and finding hobbies that interest you. Using the tips provided below will allow you to implement a sense of enjoyment during your time on campus!

  • Attend events: almost every college or university will host an org fair which highlights clubs that students are encouraged to join. Find an organization you're interested in and apply to become a member or attend their regular events if you are not ready to commit to the club.
  • Introduce yourself: whether you are commuting to class or heading back to your room, say hi to unfamiliar faces as you might become lifelong friends. Almost everyone in college is interested in making friends and having a social life. Find which introduction strategies work best for you and get to finding your group!
  • Explore campus: stroll through your college or university and find places where you'd like to study, relax, or hang out with friends. Exploring a new space can be fun because you tend to find where you belong and where you feel most comfortable.
